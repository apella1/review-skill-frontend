# Welcome to ReviewSkill

ReviewSkill is a modern spaced repetition learning platform designed to help you retain knowledge effectively. Whether you're a student, professional, or lifelong learner, our platform adapts to your learning pace.

## Key Features

- **Smart Flashcards**: Create and study flashcards with our intelligent spaced repetition system
- **Learning Notes**: Organize your study materials in one place
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor your learning journey with detailed analytics
- **Customizable Experience**: Adapt the platform to your learning style

## Getting Started

1. Create an account or sign in
2. Create your first flashcard deck
3. Start learning with our spaced repetition system

## Why ReviewSkill?

Our platform is built on proven learning science principles, helping you:

- Retain information longer
- Study more efficiently
- Track your progress systematically
